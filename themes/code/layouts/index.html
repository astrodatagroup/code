About

This is a proposal to design and convene a weeklong crash course open to astronomers of all levels to focus on “advanced” topics related to scientific software development and maintenance. The target audience would be astronomers who already develop, package, and/or contribute to scientific software and want to take their involvement to the next level. The week would include time for lectures, demonstrations, unconference sessions, and time for prototyping and co-working (“hacking”). We would primarily focus on the conceptual and social challenges associated with “production” software development, rather than specific tools/languages/etc.
